Our Policies

Returns

returns

We accept returns. You can return unopened items in the original packaging within 30 days of your purchase with a receipt or proof of purchase. Unfortunately, if 30 days or more have passed since your purchase, we cannot offer you a refund or an exchange.

Once we receive your item, we will inspect it and notify you that we have received your returned item. We will immediately notify you of the status of your refund after inspecting the item. If your return is approved, we will initiate a refund to your original method of payment. You will receive the credit within a certain number of days, depending on your card issuer's policies.

Exchanges

exchanges / fixes

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange it for the same item, please contact us at sonderbeadsco@gmail.com

You will be responsible for paying for your own shipping costs for returning your item. Shipping costs are non-refundable. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.

If your item is damaged, we can work with you to get it replaced or fixed. There are no replacements or fixes after 3 months from the date of purchase. Please contact us at [contact information] to initiate this process.

Shipping

shipping

All orders are subject to product availability. If an item is not in stock at the time you place your order, we will notify you and refund you the total amount of your order, using the original method of payment.

Items offered on our website are available for delivery to addresses in the United States. Any shipments outside of the United States are not available at this time.

Processing

processing

Unless there are exceptional circumstances, we make every effort to fulfill your order within 1-7 business days of the date of your order and 1-7 days for it to arrive to you.

If you have any questions about your order, were sent the wrong items, or have a problem with your purchase please EMAIL or DM us on Instagram

Cancellation of and order us only allowed 24 hours within purchasing

Customs

customs

All custom orders will be handled on a case-by-case basis. We will contact you to ensure that the custom item you want is accurate based on the description you provided. Please allow 1-2.5 weeks from the date of your order for fulfillment of custom items and an additional 1-7 days for it to arrive to you.
